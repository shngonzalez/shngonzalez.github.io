Animating Mochi

// Animation, concept design, storytelling

November - December 2017

Summary

Mochi is a 1-minute animated short film created as a group project for a Visual
Thinking class. It captures the journey of a seedling growing up and discovering the
world above ground.

I produced the story and concept idea, helped create a storyboard and animated 200
frames for 20 seconds of the short. I also created the video thumbnail and
synchronized music and sound effects.
